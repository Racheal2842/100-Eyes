100 Eyes Project

I am Dr Racheal Osasenaga Ohonbamu, an NYSC CORPER serving at General Hospital Odan, Lagos Island, Lagos, Nigeria. I am seeking sponsorship for my community development service project, "Project 100 Eyes: Restoring Vision, Transforming Lives"— a community-based eye care initiative in Sura Local Government, Lagos Island.

Good vision is essential for a quality life, yet many lack access to eye care due to financial barriers and lack of awareness. 100 Eyes aims to bridge this gap by providing free vision screenings, glasses, medications, and public education.

With each series, we will expand our reach—transforming lives, empowering students, and restoring visual independence to the elderly.
